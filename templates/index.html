About The Game

You've been selected to join the Cultural Event Planning Committee at a Tunisian university. Your mission is to help organize a major event showcasing various cultural traditions.

Before taking on responsibilities, you'll attend an orientation meeting where your language abilities will be assessed through natural conversations with committee members.

This interactive experience will evaluate your language skills based on the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) levels from A1 to C1.

Key Features

Interactive Dialogue

Engage in meaningful conversations with committee members to showcase your language skills.

AI Assessment

Advanced AI evaluates your vocabulary, grammar, comprehension, and fluency in real-time.

Achievements & XP

Earn experience points and unlock special achievements as you progress.

CEFR Certification

Receive a detailed assessment of your language level based on international standards.
