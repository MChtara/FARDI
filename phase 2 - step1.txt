Welcome, [Player]!

Phase 2 - Step 1: Assigning Roles
Learning Outcomes (For Implementers):
By the end of this step, students should demonstrate:
* A1 (Basic): Share simple stories about Phase 1, suggest and confirm roles with basic vocabulary, and reflect minimally on the event's cultural impact.
* A2 (Elementary): Tell short stories with basic connectors (e.g., "because"), make role suggestions with simple reasoning, and reflect with some cultural details.
* B1 (Intermediate): Narrate clear Phase 1 experiences, justify role suggestions with balanced reasoning, and reflect on roles with cultural connections.
* B2 (Upper-Intermediate): Craft engaging stories linking to Phase 1, provide detailed role justifications, and reflect fluently on the event's cultural significance.
Implementer Notes: Use these CEFR-aligned outcomes to calibrate feedback and scoring, but never display "A1," "A2," "B1," "B2," or scores to students. Present feedback as team encouragement (e.g., "Great start! Let's add more cultural details!") to maintain immersion in the cultural event planning context.
