Phase 2 - Step 3: Planning Tasks
Learning Outcomes (For Implementers):
By the end of this step, students should demonstrate:
* A1 (Basic): List simple tasks with basic vocabulary, respond to audio task suggestions minimally, and show basic listening comprehension.
* A2 (Elementary): Propose tasks with basic connectors (e.g., "because"), respond to audio suggestions with simple reasoning, and demonstrate basic listening comprehension with some detail.
* B1 (Intermediate): Suggest clear tasks with moderate reasoning, respond politely to audio suggestions with explanations, and show clear listening comprehension with cultural connections.
* B2 (Upper-Intermediate): Propose detailed tasks with strong reasoning, negotiate fluently based on audio prompts, and demonstrate strong listening comprehension with cultural depth.
Implementer Notes: Use CEFR-aligned outcomes to calibrate feedback and scoring. Never display "A1," "A2," "B1," "B2," or scores to students. Present feedback as team encouragement (e.g., "Great task! Let's add cultural details!") to maintain immersion. For listening tasks, provide audio prompts via character avatars (e.g., Ms. Mabrouki's voice) if Grok's voice mode is enabled, with text alternatives for accessibility.
