Phase 2 - Final Writing Activity: Drafting the Action Plan
Learning Outcomes (For Implementers):
By the end of this activity, students should demonstrate:
* A1 (Basic): Write a simple action plan with basic vocabulary, minimal structure, and basic listening comprehension.
* A2 (Elementary): Write a short action plan with basic connectors and some cultural details, demonstrate basic listening comprehension with details.
* B1 (Intermediate): Write a clear action plan with moderate structure and cultural connections, show clear listening comprehension.
* B2 (Upper-Intermediate): Write a detailed, cohesive action plan with precise vocabulary and strong cultural ties, demonstrate strong listening comprehension.
Implementer Notes: Use CEFR outcomes for feedback/scoring. Hide CEFR/scores. Feedback as team encouragement. Audio prompts for listening tasks with text alternatives.

The committee room is filled with excitement as the team prepares the final action plan for the cultural event. Ms. Mabrouki says, "Let's write a section of our action plan to summarize our roles, meeting schedule, and tasks!" Your job is to draft a clear, organized plan, incorporating what you hear from the team, building on Steps 1-3 and the cultural theme from Phase 1.
